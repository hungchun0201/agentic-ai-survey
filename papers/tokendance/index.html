6. Collective KV Cache Reuse

Figure 6 (from paper): Per-request (N pipelines) vs collective (1 shared pass).

6.1 Compute Amortization Math

Cost model for RoPE + key-diff analysis:

(1) $$T_{\text{PIC}}(N) \;=\; N \cdot (C_{\text{rope}} + C_{\text{kdiff}}) + \sum_{i=1}^{N} C_{\text{recomp},i}$$
(2) $$T_{\text{TokenDance}}(N) \;=\; C_{\text{rope}} + C_{\text{kdiff}} + \sum_{i=1}^{N} C_{\text{recomp},i}$$

Shared cost compressed from N× to 1×; per-agent tail stays small.

6.2 When Does This Break Even?

Wins for any N≥2; peak 2.57× at 10 agents, QPS=1.

11. Takeaways & Open Questions

Strengths

  • Collective framing.
  • Compute + storage joint design.
  • Zero-cost reconstruction.
  • Honest accuracy story.

Limitations & Open Questions

  • Requires <TTSEP> integration.
  • Narrow hardware/model footprint.
  • Assumes synchronized rounds.
  • Group-size scaling untested.

11.1 Where TokenDance Sits in the Stack

Orthogonal to PIC; amortizes across agents.

Bottom line: The design is a reframing; the numbers are consequences.
